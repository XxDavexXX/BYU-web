Chamber of Commerce of Lima - Perú

Reason: I have chosen this name to represent the chamber of commerce of the region I belong to and it reflects the purpose of promoting and supporting local businesses.

Purpose of the Site

Purpose 1: The Chamber of Commerce of Lima - Perú aims to primarily promote economic growth and business development in the region. We provide a platform for local businesses to connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Purpose 2: Additionally, our website offers up-to-date information on events, resources, and networking opportunities for entrepreneurs and members of the business community.

Scenarios

  1. An entrepreneur is interested in participating in networking events to expand their contact network. They are looking for information on upcoming events organized by the chamber.
  2. A community member wants to know who the members of the board of directors are and how to contact them to raise concerns or suggestions.
  3. A potential investor is researching population growth and business opportunities in the region. They are looking for updated demographic data.

Color Scheme

  • Color 1: Dark Blue (#00274C) - Used for titles and navigation elements.
  • Color 2: Dark Green (#00542F) - Used as an accent and for call-to-action buttons.

Typography

  • Headers: Sans-serif font (e.g., Roboto)
  • Body Text: Serif font (e.g., Lora)
